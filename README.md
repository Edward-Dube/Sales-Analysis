# Sales Data Analysis Project

## Objective
Analyze the Sample Superstore sales dataset to uncover revenue trends, top-performing products, and regional performance.
This project demonstrates data cleaning, analysis, and visualization skills using Python.

## Tools Used
- Python  
- Pandas  
- Matplotlib  
- Seaborn  

## Dataset
[Sample Superstore Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vivek468/superstore-dataset-final)  

The dataset includes:
- Orders, customers, and products  
- Sales, profit, quantity, discount, and regions  
- Data from 2014–2017

## Data Cleaning
- Removed duplicates  
- Converted `Order Date` to datetime format  
- Verified all columns have no missing values  

## Key Insights

### 1. Total Revenue
- The total revenue across all orders is **$2,297,200.86**  
- This shows a large, active customer base and significant sales volume

### 2. Sales by Category
- Technology: $836,154.03  
- Furniture: $741,999.80  
- Office Supplies: $719,047.03  

Technology is the highest revenue-generating category, followed by Furniture and Office Supplies, suggesting strong demand across all product categories.

### 3. Top 5 Products
1. Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier – $61,599.82  
2. Fellowes PB500 Electric Punch Plastic Comb Binding Machine – $27,453.38  
3. Cisco TelePresence System EX90 Videoconferencing Unit – $22,638.48  
4. HON 5400 Series Task Chairs for Big and Tall – $21,870.58  
5. GBC DocuBind TL300 Electric Binding System – $19,823.48  

These bestsellers contribute a significant portion of total sales, highlighting products for focused inventory and marketing strategies.

### 4. Sales by Region
- West: $725,457.82  
- East: $678,781.24  
- Central: $501,239.89  
- South: $391,721.91  

West and East are the highest revenue-generating regions, indicating key markets for promotional campaigns and sales strategy.

### 5. Monthly Sales Trend
- Seasonal spikes occur in Q4 (Oct–Dec), e.g., Nov 2017 reached $118,447.83  
- Lowest sales are typically in Jan–Feb, e.g., Feb 2015 only $11,951.41  

Seasonal patterns suggest opportunities for forecasting, marketing, and inventory optimization.

## Conclusion
This analysis highlights:
- High-performing categories and products  
- Key revenue-generating regions  
- Seasonal sales trends  

It demonstrates the ability to clean, analyze, and visualize business data, providing actionable insights for decision-making.
